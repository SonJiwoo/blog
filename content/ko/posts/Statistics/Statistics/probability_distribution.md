#### 정규 분포(Normal Distribution) 
$$ \text{X~} N(\mu, \sigma^2) \rightarrow f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} \exp(-\frac{(x-\mu)}{2\sigma^2}^2) $$

$$ E(X) = \mu, Var(X) = \sigma^2$$
